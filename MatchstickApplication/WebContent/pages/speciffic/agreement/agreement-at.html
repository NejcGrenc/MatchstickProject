Information and participation agreement

At the outset, we want to emphasize that your participation is voluntary. You can opt out and stop participation at any time without providing any reasons. There is no penalty for refusal on participation in this study. There is also no associated health risk with participation in the study. Since this is not a sponsored study, there will be no compensation for participation, however, we are really grateful for it.

By inputting your name and clicking the green Continue button on the login page, you agree to participate in the experiments.

Goal of the study

In the scope of this study we aim to investigate how participants’ knowledge and mental representations, generated through a series of tasks, influence transfer and thus affect their problem-solving performance in subsequent tasks.

Requirements

You can participate in this study if you are between 20 and 50 years of age. Selected age group will be filtered out afterwards, but we encourage you to participate nonetheless.

Suggested preparations

The experiments takes between 15 and 40 minutes to complete, averaging around 20 minutes. Before you start, you should remove all distractions and make yourself as comfortable and relaxed as possible. Try to perform your best during the experiment, but keep in mind to remain calm and relaxed.

Procedure

Our study is split into two parts, titled Matchstick experiment and Familiar figures experiment, which can be attempted in any order. Before attempting any of them, you will be asked in input some personal data and we urge you to provide real values. In the familiar-figures experiment, you will be asked to match similar images, and in the matchstick experiment, you will be asked to solve equations build up of matchsticks.

Personal data

Some personal data will be acquired for the purpose of the experiment and for the purpose of tracking subjects to prevent fraud. Experimental data (performance during experiments) and basic personal data [age and sex] will be stored on a secure server accessible only to experimentators. Sensitive personal data, such as IP addresses and locations, will be stored in an encrypted fashion. Subjects cannot be tracked even in a remote event of data breach. Any published data will not contain any personal information. Only aggregated data.
