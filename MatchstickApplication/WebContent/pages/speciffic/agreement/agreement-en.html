Research is split into two studies, titled Matchstick study and Familiar figures study, which can be attempted in any order. These studies are harmless and painless and should take about 20 minutes to complete. Instructions on how to participate in each study will be provided along the way. In the following text, term "subject" will be used for participant of this study. By inputting his/her name and clicking on the green Continue button on the login page, subject agrees to do participate in the experiments and try his/her best to complete both studies in a quick and calm fashion. Subjects are at any point allowed to quit and leave the study, without any reprecutions. Subjects are advised to create a calm and relaxed environment before starting any of the experiments.
