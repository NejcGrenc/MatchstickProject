Goal

This part consists of 16 tasks. In each task, there will be a picture of an object on the left (standard) and an six similar pictures only one of which is totally identical to the standard. Your goal is to select the image that exactly matches the standard as fast as you can.


Click on an image on the right to select it.

Opens this information page, but does not stop the task execution.
