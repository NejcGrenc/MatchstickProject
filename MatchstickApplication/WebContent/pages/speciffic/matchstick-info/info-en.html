Goal

By moving matchsticks from one position to another make the equation on the screen evaluate to correct.
It is important to do it in as few moves as possible and as quickly as possible.
Additionally, pay attention to the information text above the task.


{{additional_argument}}



Click on a matchstick to pick it up and move it with the mouse.
Click on a valid location to drop it.

Using scroll wheel on a mouse will rotate the picked-up matchstick.

When big red button turns to green, the equation is correct.
Click on the green button to proceed.



Buttons on the right side are:

Restarts the current equation to its original incorrect setup.

Opens this information page.
